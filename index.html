SLICKLINE SERVICES


Wireline/Slickline services are used on an existing wellbore to lower equipment or measurement devices into the well for well intervention and reservoir evaluation. Wireline/Slickline is a thin wire of 0.108” to 0.125” size that is introduced into a well to deliver and retrieve tools downhole and can support the acquisition of data from memory tools regarding the conditions of the wellbore.

COIL TUBING OPERATION


Coiled tubing applications include drilling operations, hydraulic fracturing, well completions, removing sand or fill from wellbore, and other applications that involve pumping fluids at high temperatures and high salinity.
